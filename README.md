## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Active internet connection
- Valid email address and mobile number for registration

### Usage

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account with your email and mobile number
2. **Browse Items**: View all available clothing items on the main page
3. **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific items by name, brand, or type
4. **Add to Cart**: Click "Add to cart" on items you're interested in
5. **Finalize Purchase**: Review your cart and send purchase requests to sellers
6. **List Items**: Switch to seller mode to list your own clothing items
7. **Manage**: Track your purchases, sales, and manage your listings
